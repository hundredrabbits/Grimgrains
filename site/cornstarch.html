cornstarch

Cornstarch is derived from corn (maize), it is obtained from the endosperm of the kernel. Cornstarch is used as a translucent thickener and anti-caking agent.

Cornstarch is sometimes used to fry foods, a thin outer layer of starch increases oil absorption and adds crispiness.

Weight: 1/4 cup | 1 oz | 28 g
