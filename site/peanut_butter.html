peanut butter

A spread made from dry-roasted peanuts, kinds without added sugar or oils are preferable. Peanut butter is a rich source of protein. The US consumes $800 million of this stuff annually. The flavor of peanut butter combines well with other flavors, such as oatmeal, savory sauces, and various types of breads and crackers.

peanuts

A crop grown mainly for its edible seeds! Peanuts are similar in taste and nutritional profile to tree nuts, and can be made into peanut butter. Peanuts are are rich source of protein, 1/4 cup of peanuts equals one serving of the 3-4 servings of protein required for good health.
