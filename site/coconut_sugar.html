coconut sugar

Coconut sugar has a caramel-like flavor, and is subtly sweet. It is produced from the sap of the flower bud stem of the coconut palm. It is used as a sweetener in many countries. Coconut sugar isn't highly processed, and so its flavor can vary depending on what species was used and where/when it was harvested. Coconut sugar is not essential to good health and should be consumed in moderation.
