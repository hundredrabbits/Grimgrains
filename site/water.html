water

Water is a transparent, tasteless, odorless and near-colorless chemical substance, the base for all organic life on earth.

To function properly, the body requires between 1-7L of water/per day to avoid dehydration. The precise amount depends on the level of activity, temperature, humidity, and other factors. 2.5 liters of total water daily is the minimum to maintain proper hydration.
