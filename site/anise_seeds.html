anise seeds

Anise, also called aniseed or Pimpinella, is a spice with a flavor similar to star anise, fennel and liquorice. Anise is sweet and very aromatic.It's often used to flavor foods and drinks. The seeds, whole or ground, are used for preparation of teas and tisanes.
