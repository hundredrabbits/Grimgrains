instant yeast

Yeast, the most common being S. cerevisiae, is used as a leavening agent in baking.

Instant yeast is sold in a very small granulated form, when baking it's possible to add it right into dry ingredients (no need to proof it like active dry yeast). Instant yeast absorbs water quickly, and releases more carbon dioxide which causes baked goods to rise faster and better.
