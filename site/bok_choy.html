bok choy

Bok Choy (Brassica rapa), or pok choy, is a winter-hardy vegetable, a type of Chinese cabbage. Bok Choy is a rich source of vitamin A and C, and provides some calcium.

Raw bok choy should not be consumed in large mounts over extended periods as it can inhibit uptake of iodine (ref).
