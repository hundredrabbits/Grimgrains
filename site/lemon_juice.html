Lemon juice is used in many culinary and cleaning uses.

lemon

Lemons are a citrus fruit with a distinctive sour taste. They are a rich source of vitamin C. Lemon is sometimes used as a short-term preservative on foods (apples, avocados, bananas) that brown or oxidize after being sliced.
