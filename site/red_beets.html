red beets

Red beets, or beetroots, are the taproot portion of the beet plant and have a red fleshy interior. Underneath the surface, the flesh is a deep crimson and is dense, aqueous, and crunchy. When cooked, Red beets develop a tender, soft texture with a mildly sweet, earthy flavor. The beet plant also grows leafy stalks that are edible and have a taste similar to swiss chard.It is consumed raw in salads, they can also be roasted, steamed, fried and boiled. Beetroot is used as a red food colorant in desserts, candies and a variety of other baked goods, soups and desserts, it's also used to make sauces and dips. Don't be alarmed, beetroot juice will make your stools and urine red for a day.
